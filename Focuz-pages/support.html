Frequently Asked Questions

What is elastic scoring?

Elastic scoring is Focuz's signature feature. Instead of streaks that reset to zero when you miss a day, your consistency score adjusts gradually. Missing one day might drop your score from 96% to 94% — never to zero. Your progress is always preserved.

How many habits can I track for free?

You can track up to 5 habits for free. Unlocking Focuz Pro ($9.99 one-time) gives you unlimited habits plus additional features.

Does Focuz sync across devices?

Yes! Enable iCloud Sync in Settings to sync your data across all your Apple devices signed into the same Apple ID.

Can I set flexible frequencies?

Absolutely. Focuz supports daily, X times per week, X times per month, and every-other-day frequencies. Your habit, your schedule.

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings → Upgrade to Pro → Restore Purchases. Your Pro unlock is tied to your Apple ID.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. iCloud sync uses your personal iCloud account — we never see your data. See our Privacy Policy.
