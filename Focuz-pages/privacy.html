Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 26, 2026

Introduction

Focuz ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Local Data Only: Focuz stores all your habit data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your habit data, completion records, or personal information.

iCloud Sync (Optional): If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is encrypted and only accessible through your Apple ID. We cannot access this data.

Analytics: We do not collect any analytics, tracking data, or usage statistics.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Email addresses or contact details
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Habit data or completion records

In-App Purchases

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to all transaction data.

Data Retention

All data is stored on your device. You can delete all data at any time by deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Focuz is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
