# Price Configuration

## Monetization Model
One-Time Purchase (非消耗型内购)

## App Store Pricing
- **Price**: $9.99 (USD) - or choose from App Store pricing tiers
- **Setting Location**: App Store Connect → Your App → Pricing and Availability

## App Store Connect Setup Instructions
1. Go to App Store Connect → Your App → Pricing and Availability
2. Click "Add Pricing" or edit existing pricing
3. Select price tier (e.g., Tier 10 = $9.99)
4. Save

## Implementation Notes
- NO IAP configuration required for subscription
- NO StoreKit subscription configuration required
- NO PurchaseManager for subscriptions required
- All features unlocked after one-time purchase
- Price displayed on App Store product page
- One-time non-consumable IAP for Pro features unlock
