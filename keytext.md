## Description

**Focuz — The habit tracker that doesn't punish you for being human.**

Most habit trackers use streaks. Miss one day? Your streak resets to zero. For ADHD brains, that's devastating. One slip erases weeks of effort.

Focuz is different.

🟢 **Elastic Scoring — Never Reset to Zero**
Our signature scoring system means missing one day only slightly adjusts your consistency score. 96% → 94%. Not 96% → 0%. Your progress is preserved.

⏰ **Time Visualization — See Time Passing**
Color-coded time flow bars and heatmaps help time-blind users actually see where their day went. No more "where did the time go?"

🔄 **Flexible Frequency — Not Just Daily**
3x per week? Every other day? 10x per month? Focuz supports the frequency that works for YOUR life—not a rigid daily schedule.

📊 **90-Day Heatmap**
See your consistency at a glance with a GitHub-style heatmap. Patterns emerge. Progress becomes visible.

⏱️ **Built-in Timer**
Start a 5, 15, or 30-minute timer right from your habit. No app switching needed.

🌙 **Gentle Weekly Reset**
Instead of harsh "you failed" messages, Focuz gently shows your weekly consistency and encourages you to keep going.

🔒 **One-Time Purchase — No Subscription**
$9.99 once. Forever. No monthly fees, no subscription fatigue—just a tool that works.

☁️ **Optional iCloud Sync**
Your data, across all your devices. Toggle it on or off—your choice.

**Built for ADHD brains. Designed for everyone who's ever felt defeated by a streak counter.**
